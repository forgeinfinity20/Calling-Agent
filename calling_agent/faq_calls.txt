Q: How do I call someone?
A: Simply say "Call [name]" or "Dial [number]."

Q: Can you redial the last number?
A: Yes, I can redial your last outgoing call.

Q: How do I see missed calls?
A: Ask "Show my missed calls" to list them from the call logs.

Q: How do I schedule a call?
A: Say "Schedule a call with [name] at [time]" — I’ll note it down or set a reminder.

Q: Can I record calls?
A: Recording requires permission and depends on your device and local laws.
